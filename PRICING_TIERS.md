# Forge Agency Pricing Tiers (2026-05)
_Industry standard for ROI-focused bespoke AI agencies_

## Starter Agent
*   **One-Time Build:** $1,500 – $3,500
*   **Optional Maintenance:** $250 / mo
*   **Commitment:** None — flat one-time engagement, monthly maintenance is opt-in
*   **Scope:** Single-purpose agents (e.g., Lead Qualification, Content Ingestion, Support Triage). Productized scope, fast deployment, designed to prove value before any retainer.

## Bespoke Workflow Agent
*   **Build:** $10,000 – $18,000
*   **Monthly Retainer:** $2,000 – $3,000
*   **Commitment:** 6-month minimum
*   **Scope:** End-to-end workflow automation (e.g., Full RevOps Pipeline, Automated Customer Onboarding, Content Production Engine). Multi-step agents wired into your stack with iteration, monitoring, and tuning included in the retainer.

## Multi-Agent System
*   **Build:** $35,000+
*   **Monthly Retainer:** $5,000 – $15,000
*   **Commitment:** 6-month minimum
*   **Scope:** Interconnected agent swarms, deep internal tool integration, and high-volume data reasoning. Treated as a real intelligence layer for the business — not a single workflow.

## ROI Model
Forge focuses on **Value-Based Pricing**. If a Bespoke Workflow Agent replaces a $70k/year role, a $14k build + $2.5k/mo retainer pays back inside the first year and compounds from there. The Starter Agent tier exists to prove that ROI on a smaller surface before scaling into the larger commitments.
